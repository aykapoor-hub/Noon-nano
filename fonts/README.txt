Drop Noontree font files here (served at `<base>/fonts/`):

  Noontree-Regular.woff2    (weight 400)
  Noontree-Medium.woff2     (weight 500)
  Noontree-SemiBold.woff2   (weight 600)
  Noontree-Bold.woff2       (weight 700)
  Noontree-ExtraBold.woff2  (weight 800)

.ttf is accepted as a per-weight fallback. Until files are present the app
falls back to Figtree automatically (see src/loadFonts.ts).
